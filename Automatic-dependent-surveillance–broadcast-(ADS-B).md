### Log into microSD
The ADSB RX app will log each frame including the following columns:

* **(YEAR MONTH DAY HOUR MIN SEC)**
* **(RAW PACKET IN HEX)**
* ICAO: **(ICAO)**
* **(CALLSIGN)**
* Alt: **(ALTITUDE)**
* Lat: **(LATITUDE)**
* Lon: **(LONGITUDE)**

Example:

`20171103100227 8DADBEEFDEADBEEFDEADBEEFDEADBEEF ICAO:nnnnnn callsign Alt:nnnnnn Latnnn.nn Lonnnn.nn`
